### **12. Improved Triggering Logic**

**Exit Intent:**
- Detects mouse leaving through top of page
- Only triggers once per session
- Stored in sessionStorage

**Time-Based:**
- Shows after 45 seconds (increased from 30s)
- Only if exit intent hasn't triggered
- Gives users more time to engage first

**Why 45 seconds?**
- Research shows optimal time is 30-60s
- Too early: Annoying
- Too late: Already decided to leave
- 45s: Sweet spot for engaged users who haven't converted
