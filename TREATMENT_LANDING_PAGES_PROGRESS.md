## Treatment Landing Page Template

### **Standard Structure (Established):**

1. **Metadata** - SEO keywords (English + Arabic), title, description
2. **Hero Section** - Value proposition, cost range, CTAs
3. **Quick Stats** - 4 cards (cost, success rate, savings, recovery)
4. **Cost Comparison Table** - India vs USA vs UK vs UAE (6 procedure types)
5. **What's Included** - 4 cards (pre-procedure, procedure day, healing, final)
6. **Technical Comparison** (if applicable) - Table comparing methods/approaches
7. **Top 3-5 Clinics/Hospitals** - Detailed profiles with cost, doctors, technology, why choose
8. **Recovery Timeline** - 4-6 phases from Day 1 to final results
9. **FAQ Schema** - 8 Q&A pairs for rich snippets (or 2 Q&A for streamlined pages)
10. **FAQ Section** - 4 additional questions with detailed answers (full pages only)
11. **Final CTA** - Free consultation, WhatsApp contact
